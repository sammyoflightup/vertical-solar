Our Story

More Than a Solar Company... We're Energy Advocates

Founded in 2017 through a strategic partnership between GreenStreak Energies Limited (GSEL) and Vertical Energy & Networks Ltd, Vertical Solar delivers sustainable power and energy infrastructure across Nigeria. We specialize in the design, installation, and management of solar, hybrid, and industrial power systems for commercial and institutional clients.

Customized Solutions We assess your specific needs to design a system that's perfect for you.
Premium Quality Tier-1 panels and industry-leading components for maximum efficiency and lifespan.
Long-Term Partnership Dedicated support and maintenance services beyond installation.
